MOISES CAMEO HALABE

about me

Hello! Welcome to my page. My name is Moises Cameo, a Multimedia Design student at the Universidad Anáhuac México. I’m interested in illustration, editorial design, video edition, concept art and character design. My inspirations include artists such as Alphonse Mucha, Isadora Zeferino, Remedios Varo, Bobby Merino; animation studios such as Ghibli, Wes Anderson and Pixar and literary authors such as Rainbow Rowell, J. K. Rowling, Cecelia Ahern and Mackenzi Lee.

I am interested in the multiple mythologies of ancient cultures, history, design and art. My work is oriented to show who I am as a Mexican-Jew, with all its facets and creative opportunities.
